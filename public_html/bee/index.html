Blockchain technology significantly improves the efficiency and robustness of existing business processes.

Learn how to harness this disruptive technology with our University of California, Berkeley accredited course.

This course is structured to prepare individuals to understand and work with blockchain technology through a comprehensive introduction. It is critical to have a clear understanding of the functionalities and possibilities blockchain offers in order to remain relevant in the decentralized digital age.
